About Us

Welcome to Strong Roots Public School, where we nurture young minds from Nursery to 10th grade. Located at Hyderabad Hatri Bypass, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive education that lays a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

Our Programs

We offer a range of educational programs designed to meet the needs of our diverse student body. Our curriculum includes:

  • Nursery: Early childhood education focused on play and foundational skills.
  • Primary School: Grades 1 to 5, emphasizing basic literacy and numeracy, along with social and emotional development.
  • Middle School: Grades 6 to 8, offering a broad curriculum that includes science, mathematics, language arts, and social studies.
  • High School: Grades 9 and 10, preparing students for higher education and their future careers with advanced coursework and critical thinking skills.
